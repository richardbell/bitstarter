How to use Raddar

Raddar works in your browser.

Initially, we help you set up all the items to track and then organize them into meaningful folders.

You are in control. Add or modify the trackers and filters anytime in a very simple way. Define which team members get access.

Our servers do the routine work of monitoring all sources, including:

  • Professional social media: LinkedIn, Twitter
  • Keywords: web pages across the web where keywords get mentioned
  • Website updates

Put everything on your Raddar

Raddar is up 24/7 tracking updates on:

  • People e.g. prospects or rival teams
  • Websites e.g. customers or rival sites
  • Keywords e.g. product names
All updates are kept neatly organized in folders.
